◆ bmInterlaceFlags

unsigned char USB_UVC_VS_MJPEGVideoFormatDescriptor::bmInterlaceFlags

Specifies interlace information. If the scanning mode control in the Camera Terminal is supported for this stream, this field shall reflect the field format used in interlaced mode. (Top field in PAL is field 1, top field in NTSC is field 2.): D0: Interlaced stream or variable. 1 = Yes D1: Fields per frame. 0= 2 fields, 1 = 1 field D2: Field 1 first. 1 = Yes D3: Reserved D5..4: Field pattern 00 = Field 1 only 01 = Field 2 only 10 = Regular pattern of fields 1 and 2 11 = Random pattern of fields 1 and 2 D7..6: Reserved. Do not use
